How it Works

3 simple steps

1. Book an Appointment

Schedule a virtual or in-person visit, and answer a few quick questions.

2. Meet with your Provider

Join your scheduled appointment with the secure teleconference link delivered to your inbox or come in to the clinic for your initial session with a healthcare professional

3. Receive a Treatment Plan

After your initial visit, your provider will supply you with a personalized plan and, if applicable, a prescription to be filled at the pharmacy of your choice. Any follow up appointments will be scheduled at this time
